FAQ

Fair questions.

Why not just use ChatGPT?

You can, and it will agree with you. Generic chat starts every conversation from zero and optimizes for being liked. Contramind keeps a structured record of your company and is built to argue the other side. The value is the memory plus the disagreement, not the model.

What happens to my data?

It stays yours. Answers and timeline entries are stored for your account only, never used for training, never shared. Export to Markdown or PDF anytime, or delete your data in one action.

What happens when I hit the 3-review limit?

Nothing breaks. Your brief and timeline stay fully usable, and a visible counter shows reviews remaining so the limit never surprises you. Reviews reset monthly, or Pro removes the limit for $19/month.

Is this an AI cofounder that does the work for me?

No. Execution agents write your code and your emails. Contramind does the opposite job: it stress-tests the thinking before you spend a month executing the wrong plan. You stay the founder.

Do I need to sign up to try it?

No. The 10 questions and your one-page brief require no account. You create one only if you want to save your timeline and run decision reviews.
